Lorenzo [Scott] Bertolini

I'm a PhD student in the Artificial Intelligence Reseach Group and TAG lab from the Department of Informatics of the University of Sussex. My research area lies at the intersection of Compositional Distributional Semantics, Natural Langue Inference and Representation Learning. I am particularly interested in how graph-based architectures and data-structures can be integrated and exploited to tackle phenomena like composition, inference, and built phrase and sentence representations.

Before joining Sussex, I obtain a BSc in experimental psychology at the University of Milano-Bicocca, and a MSc in Cognitive Science from the Centre of Mind and Brain Sciences (CIMeC), University of Trento. There, I have been an intern at the Reasoning and Decision Group.
